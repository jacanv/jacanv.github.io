How Candy Bars Were Invented

Candy bars were invented by Joseph Fry. He invented them in 1847.
Joseph Fry was trying to a make new candy and he did figure it out.
Soon it became a big hit around the world.
He made it with melted cacao butter, cocoa powder and sugar.

What are some of the biggest candy bar companies?

These six companies are the biggest candy bar companies in the world.

  • 1. Mars Inc.

    Located: McLean, VA

    Mars Inc. has 29 chocolate brands in total. Some of the products made by Mars Inc are: M&Ms, Snickers, Dove/Galaxy, Mars/Milky Way, Twix, 3 Musketeers.

  • 2. Mondeléz International Inc.

    Located: Deerfield, IL

    Mondeléz International Inc. has a lot of different kinds of products. Some of the products made by Mondeléz International Inc are: Cadbury, Toblerone, Chip Ahoy!, Ritz, Oreo, and Trident.

  • 3. Barcel S.A., division of Grupo Bimbo

    Located: Mexico City, Mexico

    Barcel S.A. is a Mexican company so you probably don't know a lot of the products they make. Some of the products made by Barcel S.A. are: Takis, Chip's, Hot-Nuts, Runners, Golden Nuts, and Karameladas Pop.

  • 4. Nestlé SA

    Located: Vevey, Switzerland

    Some of the products made by Nestlé SA are: Butterfinger, Crunch, KitKat, and Wonka.

  • 5. Hershey Foods Corp.

    Located: Hershey, PA

    Some of the products made by Hershey Foods Corp. are: Hershey's Chocolate Bars, Mr. Goodbar, Krackel, and Symphony.

  • 6. Ferrero Group

    Located: Alba, Italy

    Some of the products made by Ferrero Group are: Nutella, Kinder Chocolate, Ferrero Pralines, Ferrero Rocher, and Tic Tac.

The Top 3 Best Selling
Candy Bars In America

These three candy bars are the have the most sales in the United States

3.

Hershey's Chocolate Bar

The Hershey's Company was made in 1900. Milton S. Hershey wanted everyone to enjoy a milk chocolate candy bar because only the wealthy could afford chocolate. He only charged a nickel for the Hershey's Chocolate Bar. It was served in the military during World War II. Hershey makes 373 million Hershey's Chocolate Bars per year.

2.

Snickers

The company Mars makes Snickers. It was introduced in 1930. They use 99 tons of peanuts per day to make Snickers bars. They use 16 peanuts per Snickers bar. Fun fact - they named the Snickers bar after the Mars family's favorite horse.

1.

Reese's Peanut Butter Cups

Harry Reese used to work for Hershey's then quit to make his own candy called Reese's Peanut Butter Cups. He sold the candy in five-pound boxes. During the 1920s the Reeses Peanut Butter Cups became a big hit. In 1963 Hershey bought the company for $23.5 million.

Google's Kit Kat Sculpture

When Google releases a version of their Android operating system they name it after something sweet, like Jelly Bean or Ice Cream Sandwich. One version was called Kit Kat and they built a giant Kit Kat sculpture for it.
